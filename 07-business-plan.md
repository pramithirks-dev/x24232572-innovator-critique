# GajaLens — Business Plan & Marketing Campaign

## 1. Executive Summary
GajaLens is a conservation-tech startup commercialising AI-vision drone perimeter stations that prevent human–elephant conflict (HEC) on smallholder coconut, banana and paddy farms in India's Western Ghats. We sell hardware-as-a-service at ₹499/month per pod, bundle a crop-loss insurance guarantee, and monetise an anonymised herd-movement dataset to forest departments and conservation NGOs. Our wedge market is 180,000 farming households across Wayanad, Hassan and Valparai. We are raising **US $4.2M seed** to scale a 90-day pilot to 5,000 paying pods within 24 months.

## 3. Business Model

### Revenue streams
1. **Subscription** — ₹499/mo per pod (₹6k ARR)
2. **Insurance margin share** — partner ICICI Lombard, 10% of premium
3. **GajaNet Conflict API** — licensed to state forest departments (₹15 lakh/year/state) and conservation NGOs
4. **Government deployment grants** — co-funded pods under MGNREGA + state HEC mitigation budgets (₹120 Cr allocated FY26 across 4 states)

## 4. Go-to-Market

**Phase 1 (Months 0–6) — Anchor pilots**
- 50 pods across 12 farms in Wayanad (paid by Kerala Forest Dept grant)
- Co-marketing with Kudumbashree (4M-strong women's microfinance network)

**Phase 2 (Months 6–18) — Microfinance distribution**
- Bundle subscription with existing crop-loan products (Kerala Gramin Bank, NABFINS)
- Train 200 Kudumbashree field agents as "Gaja Mitra" installers (commission ₹400/install)

**Phase 3 (Months 18–36) — State scale-out**
- Replicate model in Karnataka (Hassan, Chikmagalur) and Tamil Nadu (Valparai, Coimbatore)
- Open GajaNet API to forest departments

**Phase 4 (Months 36+) — International**
- Sri Lanka (HEC kills 400 elephants/year), Kenya (Tsavo corridors), Thailand

## 9. Marketing Campaign — *"Listen, Don't Shoot"*

A 12-month integrated campaign anchored on a single emotional truth: **the elephant is not your enemy, the dark is.**

### Campaign pillars

1. **Hero film (90s)** — Shot in Wayanad. A grandfather and his granddaughter watch the dawn pod-photo of last night's matriarch on a phone. No narration. Tagline: *"Listen, don't shoot."*
2. **Photo exhibition** — *"Portraits at the Edge"* — 50 black-and-white elephant portraits captured by Sentinel Pods, exhibited in Kochi, Bangalore, Mumbai. Proceeds fund 100 free pods.
3. **Gaja Mitra Network** — Train 200 women installers as community ambassadors, paid per install + per testimonial.
4. **Forest Dept partnership** — Co-branded vehicle wraps and farm signboards: *"This farm is watched by GajaLens."*
5. **Schools program** — Free curriculum module distributed to 500 Kerala primary schools: *"My neighbour the elephant."*
6. **Diaspora crowdfund** — *"Sponsor a Pod"* — $700 sponsors one farm for a year. Sponsor receives the dawn portraits. Targets Indian diaspora and global wildlife donors.
